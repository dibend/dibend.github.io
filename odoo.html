What is Odoo?

Odoo is an all-in-one management software that offers a range of business applications forming a complete suite of enterprise management applications. It encompasses CRM, eCommerce, accounting, inventory, point of sale, project management, etc. Odoo's integrated applications are designed to meet the needs of companies, regardless of their size or budget. The flexibility of Odoo is such that apps can be added according to the growth of your company, adding one application at a time as your needs evolve and your customer base grows.
